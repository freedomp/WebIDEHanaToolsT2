Visual Design Foundations

Logo

The SAP Web IDE does not have an own logotype, it makes use of the SAP logo instead.

We have a Masterbrand strategy and we generally do not create other logos in addition to our corporate logo.
—SAP Brand Tools

In addition to the SAP logo there is a launch icon, which is shown in the HANA Cloud Platform, the About dialog of the SAP Web IDE, as well as in the browser address bar as a favicon.
