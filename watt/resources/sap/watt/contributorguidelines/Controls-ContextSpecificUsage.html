UI Controls

Context-specific Usage

The Flat theme of the SAP Web IDE makes use of two different sizes for its control set. Small controls are based on a height of 24 pixels (1.5 rem) while large controls have a height of 36 pixels (2 rem). Small controls should be used in side panes and the toolbar. Big controls are supposed to be used in the main area and overlays such as dialog and the wizard.

Main area Panes Toolbar Overlays (wizard, dialogs, pop-ups)
Small no yes yes no
Large yes no no yes

When instantiated in SAPUI5, by default the large version of a control is loaded. To switch to the small version of it, programmatically add .riverControlSmall as a CSS class name.

The two different sizes of controls help to place more weight on the main area and overlays and to visually stress the supporting role of the side panes to the editor.
