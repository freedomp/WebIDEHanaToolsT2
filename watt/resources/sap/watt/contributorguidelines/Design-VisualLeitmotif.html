Visual Leitmotif

The Organic Tree

The "Organic Tree" is a generative illustration that serves as the visual leitmotif of the SAP Web IDE. Its most prominent occurrence is the background image of the editor—but is also quoted throughout the SAP Web IDE.

The "Organic Tree" in the background of the editor area makes use of the user-specific workspace and its data to generate an individual illustration of the repository browser tree: each project in the repository browser is rendered as a representational tree, its sub-folders as branches, and its files as abstract fruits.

The “Organic Tree” also indicates errors, warnings and shared files by means of different colors. It is personal, revealing and beautiful. Over time, not only projects and their folder structures grow in size and complexity, but due to the generative nature of the illustration, also the dynamic background image. By means of the "Organic Tree" users of the Web Application Toolkit now literally can see their projects (i.e. their "garden" of Fiori apps) prosper.

Usage

Other quotations are the Blue Hero Header and the homepage.
